## Install the extension

Shadow Whisper is available as a browser extension for **Chrome**, **Edge**, and other Chromium-based browsers.

1. Visit the [Shadow Whisper download page](/download) or find it in the Chrome Web Store.
2. Click **Add to Chrome** (or **Add to Edge**).
3. Confirm the installation when prompted.

After installation, you'll see the Shadow Whisper icon in your browser toolbar.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission          | Why                                                                 |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Microphone**      | Required to capture your voice for transcription.                   |
| **Active Tab**      | Needed to insert transcribed text at your cursor position.          |
| **Storage**         | Saves your settings, history, and dictionary locally.               |
| **Clipboard Write** | Fallback method to paste text when direct insertion isn't possible. |

<Aside type="note">
  Shadow Whisper only accesses the active tab when you trigger a transcription. It does not read or
  monitor your browsing activity.
</Aside>

## Create your account

1. Click the Shadow Whisper icon in your toolbar.
2. Click **Sign In** or **Sign Up**.
3. Create your account with email or Google.
4. You're ready to start dictating.
