## Record your first transcription

Once you've [installed the extension](/get-started/installation) and signed in, you're ready to dictate.

### Step 1: Open any text field

Click into any text input — an email compose window, a Google Doc, a Slack message, a search bar, or any other text field.

### Step 2: Start recording

Press the keyboard shortcut:

- **Mac**: <kbd>Alt+Space</kbd>
- **Windows/Linux**: <kbd>Alt+Shift+W</kbd>

A floating pill appears on screen, indicating that Shadow Whisper is recording.

<Aside type="caution">
  If your browser asks for microphone permission, click **Allow**. Shadow Whisper needs microphone
  access to capture your voice.
</Aside>

### Step 3: Speak naturally

Talk as you normally would. Don't worry about filler words ("um", "uh"), false starts, or perfect grammar — the AI cleanup handles that.

### Step 4: Stop and insert

Press the shortcut again or click the pill to stop recording. Shadow Whisper will:

1. Transcribe your speech.
2. Clean up the text (punctuation, capitalization, filler removal).
3. Insert the result at your cursor position.

The whole process typically takes 1–3 seconds after you stop speaking.

## Tips for better results

- **Speak clearly** but at your natural pace — you don't need to slow down.
- **Minimize background noise** — a quiet environment helps accuracy.
- **Use a decent microphone** — built-in laptop mics work, but a headset or external mic improves quality.
- **Dictate in the language you want** — Shadow Whisper detects language automatically.
