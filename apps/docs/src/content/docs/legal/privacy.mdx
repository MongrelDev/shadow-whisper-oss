## Overview

Shadow Whisper is a voice-to-text tool. We process your audio to provide transcription and text improvement. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and your rights.

We are committed to privacy-by-design principles and data minimization. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

## Data we collect

### Account data

When you create an account, we collect:

| Data                  | Purpose                                   | Retention                  |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Email address         | Authentication and account identification | Until account deletion     |
| Name (if provided)    | Display in the app                        | Until account deletion     |
| Authentication tokens | Session management                        | Until logout or expiration |

**Legal basis:** Contract execution — necessary to provide the service you signed up for.

### Audio data

When you use Shadow Whisper to transcribe:

- Your audio is **sent to our cloud service** for transcription via speech-to-text AI.
- Audio is processed in real-time and **not stored on our servers** after transcription completes.
- The browser extension does **not** save audio files locally.

**Legal basis:** Contract execution — transcription is the core service.

### Transcription data

- **Transcribed text** (raw and cleaned) is stored **locally in your browser** via the extension's local storage.
- Transcriptions are **not sent to or stored on our servers** beyond the processing session.
- Local history can be cleared at any time through the extension settings.

**Legal basis:** Contract execution — local history is a requested feature.

### Usage analytics

We collect anonymized usage statistics to understand how the product is used:

| Data                   | Purpose                          |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| Word count per session | Personal productivity statistics |
| Session duration       | Personal productivity statistics |
| Active app/site domain | Context-aware text formatting    |
| Language detected      | Service quality                  |
| Timezone               | Display and streak calculation   |

We do **not** collect:

- Page titles or tab names
- Page content or accessibility text
- URLs beyond the domain name
- Browsing history

**Legal basis:** Contract execution (personal stats) and legitimate interest (service quality), with data minimization applied.

### Billing data

If you subscribe to a paid plan:

| Data                | Purpose                        | Processed by            |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| Payment method      | Subscription billing           | Stripe                  |
| Billing history     | Invoice and receipt generation | Stripe                  |
| Subscription status | Feature access control         | Shadow Whisper + Stripe |

We do not store credit card numbers or payment details directly. All payment processing is handled by **Stripe**.

**Legal basis:** Contract execution and legal/regulatory obligations.

### Dictionary and skills

Your personal dictionary entries, snippets, and skill configurations are stored on our servers to sync across sessions.

**Legal basis:** Contract execution.

## Data we do NOT collect

- **Page titles, tab names, or window titles** — removed for privacy.
- **Screen content or accessibility text** — never collected.
- **Browsing history or activity** — not monitored.
- **Audio recordings** — not retained after transcription.
- **Transcription content on our servers** — not stored beyond the processing session.

## Third-party services (sub-processors)

We use the following services to operate Shadow Whisper:

| Service          | Purpose                                     | Data shared                                 | Location |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Cloudflare**   | Hosting, CDN, Workers (compute), AI Gateway | Request metadata, audio (for transcription) | Global   |
| **Stripe**       | Payment processing                          | Billing data                                | Global   |
| **Google OAuth** | Authentication (if chosen)                  | Email, name                                 | Global   |

All sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements.

## International data transfers

Shadow Whisper is operated from Brazil. Some data may be processed outside of Brazil through our sub-processors (Cloudflare, Stripe, Google). These transfers rely on the sub-processors' standard contractual clauses and data protection certifications as mechanisms under LGPD Article 33.

## Data retention

| Data type             | Retention                                                         |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Account data          | Until account deletion                                            |
| Audio                 | Not retained after transcription                                  |
| Transcription history | Local only, controlled by you                                     |
| Usage analytics       | Aggregated, retained for service operation                        |

| Dictionary and skills | Until deletion by user or account deletion                        |

## Your rights

Under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD) and applicable privacy regulations, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data.
- **Correct** inaccurate data.
- **Delete** your data (right to erasure).
- **Export** your data in a portable format.
- **Revoke consent** where processing is based on consent.
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Request information** about which entities your data has been shared with.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [privacy@chainbreakertech.com](mailto:privacy@chainbreakertech.com).

## Children's data

Shadow Whisper is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
