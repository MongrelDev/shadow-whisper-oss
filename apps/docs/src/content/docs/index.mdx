## What is Shadow Whisper?

Shadow Whisper turns your voice into polished text. Speak naturally, and the app transcribes your words, cleans them up, and inserts the result wherever your cursor is — emails, documents, chat apps, code editors, or any text field in your browser.

It works as a **browser extension** for Chrome, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers.

## How it works

1. **Press the shortcut** to start recording (default: <kbd>Alt+Space</kbd> on Mac, <kbd>Alt+Shift+W</kbd> on Windows/Linux).
2. **Speak naturally** — Shadow Whisper captures your voice using your microphone.
3. **AI processes your speech** — the audio is transcribed and cleaned up, fixing punctuation, filler words, and formatting.
4. **Text is inserted** — the polished result is placed at your cursor position in the active tab.

## Who is it for?

- **Writers and content creators** who think faster than they type.
- **Professionals** who draft emails, messages, and documents throughout the day.
- **Developers** who want to write documentation, comments, or tickets by voice.
- **Anyone** who prefers speaking to typing for any reason.

## Key features

- **Voice-to-text with AI cleanup** — not just transcription, but intelligent formatting and punctuation.
- **Modes and custom prompts** — transform your speech into specific formats (emails, summaries, translations).
- **Personal dictionary** — teach Shadow Whisper your domain-specific terms and preferred corrections.
- **Snippets** — trigger phrases that expand into full text blocks.
- **History** — review and reuse past transcriptions.
- **Works everywhere** — any text field in any website or web app.
