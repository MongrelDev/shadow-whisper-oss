## What are modes?

Modes control **how Shadow Whisper processes your speech** after transcription. Think of them as writing assistants with different specializations.

By default, Shadow Whisper performs **intelligent cleanup**: it transcribes your speech faithfully, adds punctuation, removes filler words, and fixes capitalization. This is the base behavior that works for most situations.

Beyond cleanup, you can apply **transformations** that reshape your text for specific purposes.

## Base cleanup (default)

The default mode processes every transcription:

- Adds proper punctuation and capitalization.
- Removes filler words ("um", "uh", "like", "you know").
- Fixes minor grammatical issues without changing meaning.
- Preserves your vocabulary and intent.

**Input (spoken):** "so um I think we should probably move the meeting to uh Thursday because like the client won't be available on Wednesday"

**Output:** "I think we should move the meeting to Thursday because the client won't be available on Wednesday."

## Transform mode

When you have an active skill or custom mode enabled, Shadow Whisper goes beyond cleanup. The AI follows the skill's instructions to transform your text — rewriting for tone, format, or purpose.

For example, a "Professional Email" skill would turn casual speech into a properly formatted email with greeting, body, and sign-off.

## Context-aware formatting

Shadow Whisper knows which website or app you're using (based on the domain, not the page content). This helps it format text appropriately:

- In a **chat app** → shorter, conversational text.
- In a **document editor** → proper paragraphs with punctuation.
- In a **code editor** → preserves technical terms and formatting.
