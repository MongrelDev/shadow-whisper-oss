Notes surface hint.

Use this only as a soft formatting bias for faithful cleanup in a notes app. It does
not by itself mean the speaker wants a polished document. Select the `note` operation
only when the transcript is clearly a self-directed note, reminder, or small to-do
list, or the speaker asks for note shape.

- Favor scannability: short lines, short paragraphs, dictated bullets kept as bullets.
- Keep the speaker's own shorthand, abbreviations, and informal fragments — notes are
  for the speaker, not an audience.
- Preserve concrete details exactly: names, dates, times, amounts, links.
- Do not add titles, headers, tags, or structure the speaker did not dictate.
- Keep short captures short — a three-word note stays a three-word note.
