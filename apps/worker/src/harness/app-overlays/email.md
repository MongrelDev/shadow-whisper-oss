Email surface hint.

Use this only as a soft formatting bias for faithful cleanup in an email app or email
field. It does not by itself mean the speaker wants a complete email composed. Select
the `compose-email` operation only when the transcript is clearly an email draft,
reply, request, update, announcement, or the speaker explicitly asks for email shape.

- For faithful cleanup, preserve dictated greetings, closings, sign-offs, and line
  breaks. Do not invent them.
- Use readable paragraphs when the speaker dictates multiple ideas, but keep short
  fragments short when they are likely being pasted into an existing draft.
- Keep dictated bullets or numbered items as readable lists.
- Do not add subject lines, recipients, CC/BCC, attachments, corporate pleasantries,
  or a full email frame unless an operation explicitly asks for it.
